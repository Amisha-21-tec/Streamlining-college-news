Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I navigate the website?
    You can navigate the website using the main menu at the top of the page. Click on the desired category to explore news articles related to that topic.
  • How do I read an article?
    Click on the headline or thumbnail of the article you're interested in to read the full story. You can also use the search bar to find specific articles.
  • How can I submit news tips or story ideas?
    We welcome news tips and story ideas from the college community. Please visit our "Contact Us" page to find information on how to submit news tips or story ideas.
  • How do I report an issue with the website?
    If you encounter any technical issues or have feedback about the website, please contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page. We appreciate your feedback!
